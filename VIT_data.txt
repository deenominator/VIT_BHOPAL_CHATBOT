=====================================================
ABOUT VIT BHOPAL UNIVERSITY
=====================================================

VIT Bhopal University is a private deemed-to-be university located in Sehore district, Madhya Pradesh, India. Established in 2017, it is part of the prestigious VIT Group of Institutions founded by Dr. G. Viswanathan. The campus is spread across a lush 100+ acre campus in Kotri-Kalapipal, offering students a world-class learning environment away from the distractions of the city while being well-connected to Bhopal — the state capital.

VIT Bhopal is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and recognized by the Government of India. It follows the same academic excellence standards as its parent institution, VIT Vellore, which is consistently ranked among the top private universities in India.

-----------------------------------------------------
LOCATION & CAMPUS
-----------------------------------------------------

- Location: Kotri-Kalapipal, Sehore, Madhya Pradesh — approximately 60 km from Bhopal city.
- Campus Size: 100+ acres of lush greenery with modern infrastructure.
